Bitwise reduction (logical AND).

k_all(x, axis = NULL, keepdims = FALSE)

Arguments

x

Tensor or variable.
axis

Axis along which to perform the reduction (axis indexes are 1-based).
keepdims

whether the drop or broadcast the reduction axes.

Value

A uint8 tensor (0s and 1s).
